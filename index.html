Why This Initiative?

Across cities and small towns stand boarded-up storefronts, vacant homes, idle schools, and abandoned industrial buildings. These places are rich with history, people, and potential.


At the same time, many Christian communities possess capital, trades, skills, and families who want to live meaningful lives, with the purpose to build the Kingdom of God—but often lack clear pathways to deploy those gifts in ways that lead to lasting, locally owned Christ centered organizations.


Ur-BAM exists to make the connections that close this gap.

What Is Ur-BAM?

Ur-BAM is a growing network of families, practitioners, and partners committed to restoring neglected towns and inner-city neighborhoods through faithful presence, honest work, and long-term commitment. We aim to encourage and empower families to integrate their lives and businesses into needy communities in ways that keep value circulating inside the neighborhoods they serve.

01

Connect

Link entrepreneurs, tradespeople, and investors with place-based business opportunities.

02

Convene

Host gatherings and site visits to share proven models and cast vision.

03

Empower

Connect businesses with patient investors through equity and revenue-shared models.

Invitation

If you are considering moving into small towns or urban neighborhoods, Ur-BAM exists to walk with you as a community committed to businesses that build the Kingdom of God and bring value, stability, and Jesus' sacrificial love to the places they serve.
